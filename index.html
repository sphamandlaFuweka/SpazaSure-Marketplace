From authentic8

How SpazaSure was born from a mission to fight counterfeits

In 2023, Authentic8 was launched with a singular purpose: to solve one of South Africa's biggest problems — counterfeit goods flooding township retail.

The innovation was recognized by the SAB Foundation for its social impact. But realized that simply identifying fake products wasn't enough. Township retailers needed systemic economic solutions.

Today, SpazaSure builds on that foundation. We've rebranded and reimagined the platform to address the complete ecosystem: connecting spaza shops to verified suppliers, aggregating buying power, and providing the digital tools needed to compete.

Our mission remains unchanged: empower township retail through digital infrastructure that is trusted, transparent, and built for success.
